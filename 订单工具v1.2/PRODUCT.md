# PRODUCT.md — TWODRAPES Order Tool

## Register
product

## Users
Operations staff and management at a curtain/drape manufacturing company. Task-focused, efficiency-driven. Not designers — they need the tool to disappear into the workflow.

## Brand
Functional, efficient, professional. Violet (#7C3AED) accent. Density with clarity.

## Anti-references
- Bloated ERP systems with overwhelming navigation
- Complex dashboards with gratuitous charts
- Consumer-grade aesthetics on professional tools
- Decorative elements that don't serve the task

## Design Principles
1. **Density with clarity** — packed information, never cluttered
2. **Workflow-first** — every element serves a task
3. **Consistent channels** — Shopify and Amazon share UI, differ in data
4. **Progressive disclosure** — show what's needed, hide complexity
5. **Trust through precision** — numbers must feel exact, not approximate
